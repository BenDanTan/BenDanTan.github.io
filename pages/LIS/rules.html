This code of conduct represents and reflects our values of responsibility, honesty, dedication, and fun. This code abides by all state and federal laws in place, and all attendees are expected to abide by them. Attendees are expected to be in compliance with any UW-Madison and Wisconsin Union Policies.

Property:
  > All attendees are expected to treat each others property and personal items with respect and care
  > All attendees are expected to treat the provided materials with care and respect
  > No tampering with any event equipment without staff approval
  > Any provided equipment should be used only for the purposes stated in the event description, any variances will be subject to approval and/or grounds for removal

Privacy:
  > Do not share other participants’ personal information online or in person without their permission
  > In attending this event, participants agree to be recorded and are aware they will be recorded via Discord

Human Decency:
  > In attending this event you agree to treat all people (both in person and online) with respect and dignity
  > Harassment and Discrimination are automatic grounds for removal from the event
  > Refer to others according to their preferred names, pronouns, and identities
  > No photography or recording of other attendees without permission
  > No cheating in any game or event of any kind
  > No violence or threats of violence of any kind is allowed
  > No offensive remarks or language about but not limited to appearance, sex, race, gender identity, etc
  > No put-downs or making others feel bad about their skills/abilities
  > Treat everyone with kindness and patience, as everyone is not at the same skill level
  > Disruptive behavior including but not limited to excessive drinking, smoking, and stalking will not be tolerated

Discord:
  > Keep microphone muted if not speaking
  > No spamming or non-approved promotion in the chat
  > Use a recognizable name so other participants are not confused
  > Use appropriate language in both text and voice chat
  > Harassment and discriminatory language will not be tolerated and are automatic grounds for removal from the event
  > Anyone using Discord will be subject to Discord’s policies and codes

Failure to follow any of the guidelines set above will first result in a warning (depending on the level of offense), and upon further offense will result in expelling/banning the offending member

If you witness or are subject to any behavior that breaks the code of conduct, contact staff or (123) 456-7890 immediately so they can help resolve the situation and provide assistance as necessary
