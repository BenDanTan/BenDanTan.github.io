The Gardens Network

My first mobile application

Project Description

The first club I joined at the University of Wisconsin was Coding for Good. This club connects student developers to local projects focused on improving the Madison community on and off campus. At the first club meeting I was informed the club was looking for a new team leader for the new project for The Gardens Network so I volunteered and was given the position of Technical Team Lead. The Gardens Network in Dane county is an organization which rents out plots in large gardens throughout the Madison area for enthusiasts to grow their own plants. Our job is to create a mobile app for both iOS and Android in which users can check out and pay for plots directly through the app. Being a new project in Fall 2019, the team has so far focused on learning Googleâ€™s Flutter as it is the framework in which we plan on writing the app. In January we began putting the app together and we hope to have a working, deployable app by December 2020.
